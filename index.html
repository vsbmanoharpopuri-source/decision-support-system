Process

Four steps to clarity

01

Add your options

Enter the things you're choosing between — e.g., "Job at Google", "Job at Startup", "Freelance".

02

Define criteria

What matters to you? Salary, work-life balance, growth potential. Add each as a criterion.

03

Set weights & scores

Assign importance (weight) to each criterion, then score each option on a 1–10 scale.

04

See your answer

Hit Calculate. The algorithm multiplies weights by scores and surfaces the winner instantly.
